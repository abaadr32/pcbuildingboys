Our Story

We Build PCs.
For Every Person.

PC Building Boys was born out of frustration — watching people overpay for prebuilt machines that underperform. We believe everyone deserves a custom build that fits their exact needs and budget.

From students needing a reliable machine for schoolwork, to professionals demanding workstation-level power, to gamers chasing max frames — we've got a build for everyone.

Our team researches every component, checks compatibility, tracks price-to-performance, and puts together builds that actually make sense. No filler parts, no overpriced bundles. Just solid, honest PC building advice.
